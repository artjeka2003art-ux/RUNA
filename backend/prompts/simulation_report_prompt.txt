## Rules for probabilities
- Must sum to 100%
- No scenario below 10% or above 60%
- Base on SIMULATION DATA, not vibes. If blockers stayed strong in 2 out of 3 simulations, pessimistic gets more weight.
- Realistic is usually highest (35-50%) unless simulation data strongly suggests otherwise.

## Rules for narratives
- Write in user's language (Russian if graph has Russian nodes)
- Be specific — reference actual events from the simulation
- Don't moralize. Don't say "it's important to..." — just describe what happens.
- Make the person feel: "this could really happen to me"
